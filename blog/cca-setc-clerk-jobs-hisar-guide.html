CCA + SETC: Your Edge for Haryana Clerk Jobs

Government Jobs Updated 27 July 2026 8 min read

If you're preparing for a Haryana Clerk post, one qualification quietly does more work than most: Hartron's CCA (Course in Computer Application). Its standout benefit is the SETC written-test exemption for the Clerk Post — and understanding it early can save you a whole exam stage. Here's the honest, practical picture.

What SETC exemption actually means

Many Haryana recruitments require candidates to prove basic computer proficiency. For the Clerk Post, holding a recognised computer qualification like CCA can grant a written-test exemption (SETC) — meaning you don't have to sit that particular computer test. That's one less hurdle between you and the job. We cover the mechanics in detail in our SETC exemption guide.

The exemption isn't a shortcut around merit — it's recognition that you've already proven the computer skills the post asks for.

Who should take CCA for this path

CCA suits you if a Haryana Clerk-type notification is your goal and you want a government-recognised, locally available qualification. It starts from computer fundamentals, so you don't need prior experience. If you've genuinely never used a computer, a short Fundamentals of Computers (FC) course first makes CCA smoother.

How to use this properly

  1. Find the official notification for your target Clerk post (e.g. from HSSC).
  2. Read the computer-qualification clause — does it accept CCA or mention SETC exemption?
  3. If yes, do CCA in good time so your certificate is ready before you apply.

Frequently asked questions

Does CCA guarantee a government job?

No course guarantees a job. CCA gives you a government-recognised computer qualification and, for the Clerk Post, the SETC written-test exemption — an advantage in the process, not a guarantee.

What is the SETC exemption?

It's a written-test exemption for the computer portion of certain Haryana recruitments, such as the Clerk Post, granted to holders of a recognised computer qualification like CCA. Always confirm it against the official notification for your post.

Can I do CCA as a complete beginner?

Yes. CCA starts from computer fundamentals. If you have never used a computer, the short Fundamentals of Computers (FC) course first will help.

How long is CCA and what does it cost at Hartron Hisar?

CCA is 52 weeks and costs Rs 20,000, or Rs 18,000 as a one-time lumpsum.
