CCA vs CDA vs CCBA: Which Course Is Right for You?

Course Comparison Updated 25 June 2026 7 min read

These three courses come up in almost every counselling conversation at Hartron Hisar — and it's easy to mix them up. They overlap, but they're built for different goals. Here's the straight comparison so you can decide in a couple of minutes.

The one-line version

  • CCA — general computer skills for office & clerk jobs, with SETC written-test exemption for the Clerk post.
  • CDA — computers plus accounting, Tally and GST, built for accounting careers.
  • CCBA — a shorter, business-focused blend of computer basics and accounting.

Note: All three are NCVET-approved and NSQF-aligned. Only CCA is specifically recognized for the SETC written-test exemption for the Clerk post.

CCA — for office & clerk jobs

CCA is the flagship for government-office aspirants. It covers computer fundamentals, MS Office, typing, internet, introductory programming and web basics, capped by a project and viva. Its biggest draw is the SETC written-test exemption for the Clerk post — read the full explanation in our SETC exemption guide. Choose CCA if your target is clerical, data-entry or general office work.

CDA — for accounting careers

CDA takes the same computer foundation and adds serious accounting: fundamentals and principles of accounting, the Indian financial system, financial-statement analysis, taxation (Income Tax & GST), and Advance Tally with GST, plus an OJT project. Choose CDA if you want to work as a junior accountant, Tally operator, GST-filing assistant or accounts executive.

CCBA — the faster business-focused option

CCBA is half the length (26 weeks) and combines computer basics with accounting tailored to business operations. It's a strong pick if you want practical, business-ready skills quickly and don't specifically need the year-long depth of CCA or CDA.

How to decide in 30 seconds

  • Want a government clerk job? → CCA (for the SETC exemption).
  • Want an accounting / Tally career? → CDA.
  • Want business skills faster, with less time and lower fees? → CCBA.
  • Still unsure, or a complete beginner? → start with FC (Fundamentals of Computers), then upgrade.
A reminder on government posts: recruitment rules can change between cycles. If you're choosing CCA for a specific clerk vacancy, confirm the requirement against the official notification (for example, from HSSC) before enrolling. We're happy to help you check — call 92158-38058.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between CCA and CDA?

CCA focuses on general computer skills for office and clerk roles and carries the SETC exemption for the Clerk post. CDA adds accounting, Tally and GST on top of the computer base, for accounting careers. Both run 52 weeks.

Is CCBA shorter than CCA?

Yes. CCBA is 26 weeks; CCA and CDA are 52 weeks. CCBA is a faster, business-focused combination of computer basics and accounting.

Which course should I choose for a government clerk job?

CCA — it is recognized for SETC written-test exemption for the Haryana Clerk post. Always confirm against the official notification for your recruitment cycle.

Which course is best for an accounting career?

CDA, because it includes Advance Tally with GST and financial accounting. CCBA is a shorter alternative if you want a quicker route.
