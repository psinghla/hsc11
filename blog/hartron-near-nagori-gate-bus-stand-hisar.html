The Original Hartron in Hisar — Right by the Bus Stand, Since 1999

There are now several Hartron centres across Hisar — which is great, because it means more students can access government-recognized computer training. But if you're deciding where to enrol, location matters more than people expect: the easiest centre to reach is the one you'll actually attend regularly, on time, for the full course. On that count, the original Hartron Skill Centre — between Nagori Gate and the Bus Stand — is the most convenient in the city.

Right beside the Bus Stand — the single biggest advantage

Our centre sits between Nagori Gate and the Hisar Bus Stand, near Gurudwara on Parijat Chowk Road — in the heart of the city. For a computer institute, being next to the bus stand isn't a small thing; it's the difference between an easy daily commute and a daily struggle. If you travel in from Hansi, Adampur, Barwala, Uklana, Agroha, Narnaund, Balsamand, Sisai, Nalwa or any nearby village, you step off your bus and you're practically here. No extra autos, no long walk, no getting lost in residential lanes.

The best institute is the one you can reach without effort. Beside the bus stand, in the centre of Hisar — that's an advantage you feel every single day of your course.

The original, established since 1999

This is the long-established Hartron centre in Hisar — serving the city since 1999. That's over two decades of teaching, more than 20,000 alumni, and faculty who have guided students into government offices, banks, accounting firms and IT roles across Haryana. Experience like that isn't something a newer branch can claim overnight.

Same government recognition — what actually sets a centre apart

Here's the honest part: all Hartron centres carry the same NCVET approval and NSQF alignment. The certificate's recognition is the same. So when you're choosing between Hartron centres, the real differentiators are three things:

  • Location & access — can you get there easily, every day? (Ours: beside the bus stand, dead-central.)
  • Experience & track record — how long have they been teaching, and with what results? (Ours: since 1999, 20,000+ alumni.)
  • Guidance & support — counselling, flexible batches, placement help. (Ours: morning, afternoon and evening batches, free counselling, placement assistance.)

Easy to find — here's how to reach us

We're simple to locate, which matters when you're visiting for the first time:

  • Landmark: Between Nagori Gate & Bus Stand, near Gurudwara, Parijat Chowk Road.
  • By bus: Get down at Hisar Bus Stand — we're a short walk away.
  • Walk-in hours: Monday to Saturday, with morning, afternoon and evening batches.
